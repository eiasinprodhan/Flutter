About Me

I'm a dedicated infant care specialist with a passion for nurturing the youngest members of our community.

With over 8 years of experience working with newborns and young infants, I understand the unique needs of babies and the concerns of new parents. I hold a degree in Early Childhood Education from State University and maintain current certifications in infant CPR, first aid, and safe sleep practices.

I believe every child deserves a safe, loving environment where they can explore, learn, and grow at their own pace. My approach combines evidence-based care practices with genuine affection and patience.
